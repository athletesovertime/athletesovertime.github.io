1980's

Well known athletes were in their prime including Mike Tyson, Magic Johnson, and Joe Montana. Tables were starting to turn as this was the start of Muhammad Ali's downfall and was no longer considered the "Greatest of All Time". His match with Larry Holmes at age 38 proved that things were changing as he was knocked out for the first time.
