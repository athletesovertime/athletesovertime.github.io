1920's




The 1920's was known as the Golden Age of sports. The interest in sports
increased as more people began to pay attention The media played a big part in this
while sports gained more publicity and promotion.The most popular sports in that
decade include baseball, boxing, and college football. Along with this famous
athlete Babe Ruth dominated this decade
in being one of the most famous athletes in baseball history. There were other
many famous athletes of the 1920's including Jack Dempsey, Johnny Weissmuller,
Helen Wills, 'Red" Grange, Gertrude Edlerle, Joe Lewis, and Satchel Paige.
Majority of these athletesare male while during this time period sports were male
dominated. Competitive and athletic women wereseen as an unattractive trait.
There were some exceptions for women to play sports such as archery, tennis,
croquet and golf, but anything more intense or competitive was not acceptable.
