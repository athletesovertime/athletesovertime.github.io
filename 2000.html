2000's



The 2000's sports industry was filled with new achievements, players and controversy.
While NFL players arrests were common, most marquee players stay away from off the field
problems. But on Jan 31st, Ray Lewis, a superstar linebacker of Baltimore Ravens was involved
in a bar fight and was indicted on charges of murder. In this decade we see an introduction
towards well known athletes today. Including Tiger woods;(a professional golf player) who won
the Laureus World Sportsman and Sportswomen of the year award in 2001. Marion Jones (former world
champion in track and field) also won the award but later on admitted that she used performance
enhancing drugs. Along in the 2000's Usain Bolt gained his titles as the fastest man alive.
He broke the world records both in the 100 and 200 meter dashes at the Beijing Olympics.
Ironically, Usian later broke his own world record by winning 200 meter event in 19.19 seconds
and 100 meter in just 9.58 seconds. Technology also improved overtime allowing an imporvment in sorts aswell.
A tool called "Eye Hawk" changed sports like tennis with a ball tracking software.
